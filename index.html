IRON MAN

about

Iron Man, also known as Tony Stark, is one of the most iconic superheroes in the Marvel Comics universe. Created by writer Stan Lee, developed by Larry Lieber, and designed by artists Don Heck and Jack Kirby, Iron Man made his first appearance in "Tales of Suspense" #39 in 1963. Tony Stark, the alter ego of Iron Man, is a billionaire industrialist and genius inventor. After being critically injured and kidnapped during a weapons demonstration, he creates a powered suit of armor to save his life and escape captivity. He then further refines the suit and uses its technology to fight crime and protect the world as Iron Man.
The character of Iron Man has evolved significantly since his creation, with various iterations and storylines in the comics. He is known for his wit, intelligence, and technological prowess, as well as his struggles with his own personal demons and moral dilemmas. Over the years, Iron Man has become a central figure in the Marvel universe, featuring prominently in various comic book story arcs, animated television series, video games, and, most notably, the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU).
In the MCU, Robert Downey Jr. portrays Tony Stark/Iron Man, starting with the first Iron Man film in 2008. Downey's portrayal has been widely acclaimed, and the character has played a pivotal role in the development of the MCU, serving as a founding member of the Avengers and playing a significant part in major storylines, including the "Avengers" films and the "Captain America" trilogy. Iron Man's character arc in the MCU is marked by significant growth, sacrifice, and heroism, ultimately culminating in a dramatic and emotional finale in "Avengers: Endgame" (2019).

stark-industries

Stark Industries is a fictional multinational industrial company in the Marvel Comics universe, most prominently featured in Marvel's Iron Man and The Avengers series. It was founded by Howard Stark, the father of Tony Stark, also known as Iron Man. In the storyline, Stark Industries initially started as a small company that produced electrical appliances and munitions during World War II.
Under the leadership of Tony Stark, the company expanded its focus to include cutting-edge technology, particularly in the fields of defense, aerospace, and advanced engineering. Tony Stark's innovative genius and his development of the Iron Man suit significantly advanced the capabilities of Stark Industries, making it a leading technology company in the Marvel universe.
Stark Industries is often depicted as a major defense contractor, providing high-tech weaponry and equipment to the military and law enforcement agencies, as well as pioneering research and development in clean energy and other technological advancements. Throughout various Marvel storylines, the company's involvement in global conflicts and its contributions to technological progress play significant roles in shaping the Marvel Cinematic Universe.

skills

Micro Rockets

Micro Rockets are the first upgrade you're going to want for an Iron Man build, specifically if you're looking to do a ranged combat-focused build for Tony Stark. The Micro Rockets are an upgrade to rockets that allow Iron Man to fire off a small missile that doles out large damage.
Scorched Earth

Scorched Earth is another ranged attack upgrade to Iron Man's rocket abilities. It also requires that Micro Rockets are already unlocked.
Javelin

The sticky rockets attach to foes and detonate after a short period of time. The longer that the heavy attack button is held, the more rockets that are fired.
Arc Extension

The Arc Extension skill upgrade is an improvement on Iron Man's Arc Overload default ability. Arc Overload automatically fills Iron Man's Overcharge energy, shocks nearby foes, and increases ranged attack power and attack speed for 10 seconds.
Arc Field

The Arc Field upgrade is another improvement on the Arc Overload skill, and it requires Arc Overload Specialization I to be unlocked.
Energy Pulse

Energy Puls could be an especially powerful upgrade with a ranged build, as it helps Iron Man protect himself should an opponent get too close to him. The Energy Pulse skill is an upgrade to Iron Man's base Energy Shield ability.
Energy Shunt

Energy Shunt is a further improvement to the Energy Shield ability as well as the Energy Pulse ability. Naturally, it requires Energy Pulse to be unlocked as a prerequisite.
Ranged Combat Mastery

Ranged Combat Mastery is the perfect topper to all of these abilities, as it boosts ranged attack damage by 15%. All of these dangerous rocket attacks become even more dangerous, meaning Iron Man is even better at knocking out the hordes of foes that will be coming his way.
Smart Rockets

Another rocket upgrade that requires that the Micro Rockets are already unlocked, the Smart Rockets ability is an especially powerful upgrade, though it also uses Iron Man's Supercharge energy.
